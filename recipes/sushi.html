Salmon Sushi Recipe

Description

Homemade Asian Sushi in less than 10 minutes. want to do it yourself? countinue on reading!

Ingredients

  • 1kg of salmon
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cucumber
  • 2 carrots

Steps

  1. Cut the salmon into cubes and cook for 10 minutes in 120 degrees (celsius) oven
  2. cook the rice for 10 minutes in hot water pot
  3. cut the onions, cucumber and carrots into cubes
  4. close it all together in a circle way
  5. viola! you got yourself a Traditional Asian Sushi. bon apetit!
