Hamburger Recipe

Description

Homemade American Burger in less than 10 minutes. want to do it yourself? countinue on reading!

Ingredients

  • 1kg of meat
  • 3 tomatos
  • 1 onion
  • 2 spoons olive oil
  • 3 spoons of sea salt

Steps

  1. Cut the half of the onions to cubes and put in on a hot frying pan after putting one spoon of olive
  2. Wait for the onion to golden, then add the meat for 3 minutes on a hot frying pan
  3. Cut the onions and the tomato while waiting for the meat to burn
  4. Add 2 spoons of sea salt
  5. Mix it all and wait 2 minutes
  6. viola! you got yourself an old fasion american burger. bon apetit!
